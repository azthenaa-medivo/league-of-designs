What's League of Designs about ?

My name is Artemys, and I'm fascinated by League of Legends' (or rather, Riot's) take on design and balance. Because a lot of answers to our everyday questions (such as why Irelia hasn't been reworked in years) can be found on the Official Boards, I built this hub that gathers those Red Posts for you to read more easily. Have a good day ! (That was a tl;dr.)

What can we expect from League of Designs ?

A very convenient way to browse Red Posts. For starters :

  • A hopefully comprehensive list of all Red Posts from Riot's Boards. It's a bit like The Red Tracker you find on various websites (and the official one), with the added benefit of being sorted by champions. So you can check what CertainlyT is secretly planning for Teemo. (That's a totally innocent example.)
  • The goal is to help understand Riot's stances on champion design. League of Legends is only at the dawn of its potential awesomefulness (!!), it's a great time to learn why Irelia will be reworked and why you can't just nerf/buff stuff and get away with it. (Another utterly unbiased example.)
  • EASTER EGGS
  • In the future, I hope to add a section about items. They're equally important to the game, and we are only starting to see more good iterations of them. I haven't studied the matter as much as I have done with Champions though.

  • I will probably use this website as a blog - them Articles -, but if you want to contribute, you can send me a mail at azthenaa (dot) medivo (at) gmail (dot) com.
